Who We Are

Leaders in Japanese Translation
Services

Silver Bridges Translations Int'l (SBTI) is the Pacific Rim's quality leader in Japanese-English translation and interpretation services. For nearly three decades, SBTI's technical depth and uncompromising commitment to accuracy have made it the translation firm of choice for Fortune 500 companies, the region's leading law firms, and a host of government agencies in both the U.S. and Japan.

Document Translation

SBTI provides Japanese-English document translation services to businesses, law firms, government agencies and non-profits organization. We are equipped to handle any translation task no matter how large or complex. We are qualified to translate documents for court filing, Hague service, and immigration. Our translations are always backed by a Certificate of Accurate Translation. We also offer document summary services and independent reviews of third-party translations.

Oral Interpretation

Since 1994, SBTI has served the interpreting needs of the business and legal community in Hawaii, Japan and beyond. We have a wealth of experience in a broad spectrum of industries and disciplines ranging from general business and law to finance, taxation, real estate, construction, medical/pharmaceutical, and hotel/hospitality. We offer consecutive and simultaneous interpreting services both in-person and online/telephonic.

Deposition/Trial Interpreting

Legal interpreting is a specialized area of practice that requires the highest level of ethics, accuracy, and technical competency. SBTI provides legal interpreting services to dozens of the world's leading law offices and has interpreted in thousands of depositions and trial proceedings in jurisdictions across the U.S. We also offer online deposition interpreting, transcript translation services, and interpreter monitoring services.

Professionalism and Responsiveness

At SBTI, we understand that our clients turn to outisde translators in order to accomplish specific objectives. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and to formulate appropriate translation strategies, schedules, and delivery modalities. We offer expedited service and accept most major credit cards. Contact us for a free and confidential consultation.

Additional Services/Languages

In addition to Japanese, SBTI offers document translation services in Chinese, Korean, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, and German. We also offer foreign language training and expert witness services. Contact us for a complementary price quote.
